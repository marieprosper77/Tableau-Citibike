2017

For this section, you must take the results with caution, due to the following reasons:

Age was calculated with the Birth Year given by the user.

When the Birth Year was missing, the median age for the same gender was used.

After finishing the calculations, for some cases, the information is not credible. See the maximum value for age in the graph.

Also, in the Citi Bike System Data there is a note stating that data has been processed to remove certain kind of trips, however, there were trips with more than 3 hours of duration and even some lasted a full week, but to a service station.

For Subscribers, the situation is more manageable because the Citi Bike Program can implement controls to validate the Birth Year and the overall average trip duration was 14 minutes, even considering the outlier with 127 years old and 82 minutes for trip duration.

Remember that most of the trips were made by Subscribers, so it is important to notice that the average duration of the trip did not change by age.

2018

For this year, there was no missing data for the Birth Year and Age was also calculated with the Birth Year given by the user.
After finishing the calculations, for some cases, the information is not credible. See the maximum value for age in the graph.

To discover the real trip duration for Customers, the Citi Bike Program must exclude trips that were made to service stations or those trips with technical difficulties.
