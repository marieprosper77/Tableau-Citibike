2017 - 2018

In 2017, 16'364,657 trips were made. The month with less trips was January (726,676), and the month with more trips was October (1'897,592).

In 2018, 17'548,339 trips were made. The month with less trips was also January (718,994) and the month with more trips was August (1'977,177).

Taking each month of 2017 as the baseline for comparing ridership growth for 2018, it was observed that March of 2018 was the month with the highest increment in ridership (34%)

In 2018, they were 1'183'682 more trips than in 2017, representing an increment of 7.23%.

Trips made by Subscribers, represented the 87% of this increment, the remaining percentage (13%) were made by Customers.

Also, in this increment the proportion of trips made by Short-term Customers was slightly higher than those made by Annual Subscribers (8.3 vs. 7.1%).

The female ridership increased from 3'714,988 trips in 2017 to 4'092,462 in 2018, representing an increment of 10%.
