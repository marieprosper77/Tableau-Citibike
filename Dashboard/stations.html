2017

The top 10 stations for starting a journey were those located near mass transit stops like Grand Central, Penn Station, and Port Authority Bus Terminal.
The sum of trips starting in these stations, represented only 6% of total trips.

The top 10 stations for ending a journey were almost identical to the top 10 stations for starting a journey, except for the last one.
The sum of trips starting in these stations, represented only 7% of total trips.


Explore the changes over time by selecting the filters located on the right side of the map. If you want to know which stations did not have trips, select No Trips instead.

Most of the bottom 10 stations for starting or ending a journey were not registered on the official catalog, however the reason why they were at the bottom could be inferred by reading the name of the station (e.g. depot, test, don't use).

2018

The top 10 stations for starting or ending a journey were also those located near mass transit stops like Grand Central, Penn Station, and Port Authority Bus Terminal.
The sum of trips starting or ending in these stations, represented only 6% of total trips.
